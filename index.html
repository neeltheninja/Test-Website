7-Day Bengali Diet Plans for Weight Loss

Designed for a 25-year-old woman, 150 cm, 65 kg (BMI 28.9), these plans aim to reduce belly fat and bloating in one week using Bengali ingredients, avoiding fish and chicken. Choose between the Balanced Plan (~1,200 kcal/day, sustainable) or the Extreme Plan (~800 kcal/day, intense). Consult a doctor before starting, especially for the Extreme Plan.

Balanced Plan Guidelines

  • Calories: ~1,200/day (safe deficit from ~1,700 TDEE).
  • Hydration: 8-10 glasses water/day to reduce bloating.
  • Avoid: Fried foods, sweets, high-sodium dishes.
  • Exercise: 30 min brisk walking (~150-200 kcal burned).
  • Sleep: 7-8 hours/night for metabolism support.
  • Portions: Use smaller plates to control intake.

Balanced Plan Notes

Expect ~0.5 kg weight loss and reduced bloating for a flatter belly. Monitor energy levels and consult a doctor for prolonged use.

⚠️ CAUTION: This 800-calorie plan with intense exercise is extreme. Consult a doctor before starting. Stop if you feel faint or unwell.

Extreme Plan Guidelines

  • Calories: ~800/day for rapid results.
  • Hydration: 4 liters water/day to flush water weight.
  • Avoid: Sugary drinks, alcohol, caffeine, fried foods, sweets, high-sodium dishes.
  • Exercise: 40 min HIIT (e.g., burpees, 1 min on/off) + 20 min fasting cardio (brisk walking before breakfast). Burns ~500-600 kcal/day.
  • Sleep: 8+ hours/night to boost fat-burning hormones.
  • Stress: 10 min meditation/day to lower cortisol.
  • Post-Plan: Transition to 1,200-1,500 calories/day to avoid rebound gain.

Sample Recipes

Chirer Pulao

100g flattened rice, 50g veggies (peas, carrots), 1 tsp mustard oil, turmeric, cumin. Rinse rice, sauté spices and veggies, add rice, cook until soft. ~200 kcal.

Moong Dal Soup

50g moong dal, 1 tsp mustard oil, turmeric, cumin, 1 tomato. Cook dal with spices and tomato until soft. Blend for a smooth soup. ~200 kcal.

Tofu Stir-Fry

100g tofu, 100g mixed veggies (carrots, beans), 1 tsp mustard oil, ginger, garlic. Sauté tofu and veggies with spices. ~200 kcal.

Paneer Tikka

50g paneer, yogurt, spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander). Marinate paneer, grill or bake. ~150 kcal.
